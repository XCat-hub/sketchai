FAQ

Common questions about Sketch AI.

Do I need a Google account?

Yes. Google Sign-In is required to use AI generation features, manage your quota, and sync subscription status.

How often does my AI quota reset?

Free users receive 3 AI generations per month. Plus members receive 100+ generations per month. Your quota resets at the start of each billing period.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open Google Play → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Sketch AI, then tap Cancel. You keep Plus benefits until the current period ends.

Where is my artwork stored?

Drawings are stored locally on your device by default. Plus members can optionally back up to Google Drive. AI-generated images on our servers are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

Is the app safe for children?

Sketch AI includes built-in NSFW content filtering. The app is intended for users aged 13 and older.

Which languages are supported?

The app supports 15+ languages including English, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Spanish, French, German, and more.
